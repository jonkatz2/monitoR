monitoR

An R package for automated and manual location of sounds in digital recordings

monitoR contains:

  1. A full-featured spectrogram viewer
    • Browse spectrograms in an R graphics device
    • Annotate sounds of interest
  2. Functions to create, modify, save, and use templates for automated detection
    • Methods for selecting points
    • Plotting templates
    • Writing/reading templates
    • Comparing template performance
  3. Two automated template matching algorithms
    • View, verify, and extract results
    • Write and read templates and detections locally
  4. Functions to evaluate the automated detection process
    • Comparing manual annotations to automated detection
